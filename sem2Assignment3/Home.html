The Confinement Museum

Confinement changes our relationship with space, whether we live alone, in a couple, with roommates or in a family. Each of us develops strategies to get through the crisis by creating inner worlds. What happens to our relationship with the outside world and each other in this situation? Does it only exist through a screen, a window or a half-open door?

Definition of Confinement

: an act of confining
: the state of being confined

Ontario Enacts Declaration of Emergency to Protect the Public

March 17, 2020 9:13 A.M. Office of the Premier

Today, the Government of Ontario announced that it is taking decisive action by making an order declaring an emergency under s 7.0.1 (1) the Emergency Management and Civil Protection Act. In doing so, Ontario is using every power possible to continue to protect the health and safety of all individuals and families.
