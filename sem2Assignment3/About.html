Who We Are

Written by 自粛委員会 on 3 July 2020.

We are group of collaborative artists who have decided to commemorate in this unprecedented time in our two million years as a species.

Owing to the outbreak of the novel 2019 Coronavirus, a large number of the world's musuems were forced to close; and with it, removed the chance for many visual artists to showcase their work. With this in mind, the museum hopes to provide many aspiring and established artists a space for them to exhibit the product of their craft.

We invite you to join us in our celebration of art.
